McLaren Group

Visual Exploration

Introduction

Formula 1 and McLaren will enter a new era in the coming years, the former through a raft of sporting and technical regulation changes, the latter through the ending of their long relationship with Mercedes Benz – and moving to Honda power. This seems like a perfect time to re-evaluate the McLaren brand as a whole, progressing the visual language to remain engaging whilst encapsulating their endless desire for technical innovation and perfection.

My proposal is very much an evolution of the existing brand, pushing its best qualities forward, but injecting the heritage from when Bruce McLaren first joined the Formula 1 community in 1958.

The examples in this brand specimen are a cross section of areas the visual language can expand into. I see this as an ongoing project that will continue to develop as McLaren continues to innovate and move into new markets.
